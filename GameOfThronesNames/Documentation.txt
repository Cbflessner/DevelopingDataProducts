Impact of Game of Thrones on US Baby Names

The HBO show Game of Thrones has become a pop culture phenomenon and one of the most watched television shows of all time.
The attached app invesigates its effects on baby names in the United States, based on information taken from the Social Security Administration.
To safeguard privacy the Social Secuirty Administration restricts names to those with at least 5 occurances.  

To use the app simply check one or multiple Game of Thrones characters names and see a historgram of how often the name has appeared across the
years with an vertical line to indicate when the first season of Game of Thrones premiered.
